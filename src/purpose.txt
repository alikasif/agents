
    Find the list of companies which have posted more than 20% YoY profit and are listed in NSE. The market cap for these companies is must be below Rs.5000 crores.

    Filter them on the basis of more than 15% YoY increase in stock price

    Return the result based on quality, growth, valuation, momentum

    Quality: Higher return ratios, efficient capital management, healthy balance sheet, etc.

    Growth: Strong growth in overall topline and bottom line numbers in recent and past five years. Growth in book value is also considered.

    Valuation: An array of valuation metrics such as P/E , P/B , dividend yield, PEG, etc.

    Momentum: The Strength of the upward and consistent price direction.
